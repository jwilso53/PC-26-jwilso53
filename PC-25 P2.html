San Fransisco and Kansas City

Large cities have seen drug overdose increases, especially cities like San Fransisco and Kansas City.

San Fransisco has seen a large increase in drug overdoses in all counties. They have also seen a sharp surge in the usage of Fentanyl, therefore also increasing fentanyl overdoses.

Kansas City has also seen a large increase in drug overdoses. However, this may be linked to an increase in population, as Kansas City has prospered in the last 50 years in population and economy.
